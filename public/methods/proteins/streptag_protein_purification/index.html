Procedure

Preparations

  1. Prepare and pre-cool 10 mL Tris-HCl-NaCl-MgCl2 bacterial lysis buffer as a equibrilation and wash buffer per 5 mL bacterial lysate from 500 mL bacterial culture volume (or 10 mL buffer W).
  2. Prepare and pre-cool 1 mL biotin elution buffer per 5 mL bacterial lysate from 500 mL bacterial culture volume (or 1 mL buffer BXT).

Equilibrating the Column

  1. Place the column onto a stand in the cool room @ 2-8 ¬∞C.
  2. Remove the top cap.

  4. Equilibrate the column with 1 column bed volume (200 ¬µL) of wash buffer (or buffer W).
