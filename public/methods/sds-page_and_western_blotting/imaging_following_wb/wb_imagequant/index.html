Procedure

Heading 1

  1. Prepare the developing solution consisting of stable peroxide and luminol/enhancer 200 ÂµL each.
  2. Add the membrane to a transparent plastic wrap and remove all liquid with absorbing paper or by closing and opening the plastic and removing the liquid on the plastic.
  3. Pipette 400Âµl developing solution onto the membrane and close the plastic to ensure equal distribution.
  4. Incubate the developing solution on the membrane for 5 minutes.

Membrane Storage
  1. Place the membrane in a transparent plastic wrap.
  2. Add a small volume of PBSTTBST to keep the membrane moist.
  3. Store @ 4 Â°C for up to a week.
