Preparations

  1. Prepare one LB-agar Plate without any antibiotics.
  2. Prepare 110 mL of LB medium.

Plating Bacteria

  1. Choose a desired strain of Escherichia coli cells.
  2. Plate a one shot directly on a LB-agar Plate.
  3. Let the bacteria grow over night @ 37 ¬∞C.

Pre-culture

  1. Transfer 1 mL of the 110 mL LB medium into a photometer cuvette for the zero value.
  2. Transfer 5 mL of the 109 mL LB medium into a sterile 15 mL falcon tube.
  3. Pick a suitable clone with pipette tip.
  4. Drop pipette tip into the 5 mL of LB medium.
  5. Incubate pre-culture @ 37 ¬∞C and shaking @ 250 rpm for approximately 2‚Äì4 h until an OD600 ~ 0.4‚Äì0.8 (best 0.6) is reached or incubate @ 30 ¬∞C and shaking @ 250 rpm overnight.

Preparations Before Harvesting

  1. Prepare 50 mL of Inoue transformation buffer from your 10x stock or by supplementing 50mL of PIPES (or alternatively MOPES) buffer 10 mM (pH 6.8 with acetic acid) with MnCl2 55 mM, CaCl2 15 mM and KCl 250 mM and cool on ice until further use.
  2. Prepare 10 mL of Inoue storage buffer by supplementing 8 mL of Inoue transformation buffer with 750 ¬µL of DMSO (7.5 %) and 1250 ¬µL of Glycerol 85% (10.625 %) and cool on ice until further use.
  3. Pre-cool 80 x sterile 1.5 mL reaction tubes.
  4. Pre-cool a centrifuge to 4 ¬∞C for harvesting @ 3000‚Äì6000 g.

  2. Harvest cells by centrifugation @ 3000‚Äì6000 g and @ 4 ¬∞C for 10 minutes.
