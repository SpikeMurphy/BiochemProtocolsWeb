Procedure

Preparation

  1. Transform a suitable strain of bacteria and plate transformed bacteria on a LB-agar Plate containing appropriate selection markers (Antibiotic, X-Gal).
  2. Prepare 6 mL of sterile LB medium per clone.
  3. Prepare additional 500 mL of sterile LB medium in a 1 L Erlenmeyer flask per clone.
âš ï¸ Adjust volume as needed. The Erlenmeyer flask should be able to hold at least double the volume of LB medium used to allow for sufficient aeration.
