## Procedure

### Preparations

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
1. {{< checkbox >}} Prepare 25 mL of blocking buffer per membrane by dissolving <span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="primary">1.25 g</span><span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">1.25 g</span> of <span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="primary">BSA</span><span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">milkpowder</span> in 25 mL of <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> for a final concentration of <span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="primary">5 %</span><span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">0.05 g/mL</span>.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
2. {{< checkbox >}} Prepare 25 mL of antibody dilution buffer per two membranes by mixing 1 mL of blocking buffer with 24 mL of <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> for a final <span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="primary">BSA</span><span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">milkpowder</span> concentration of <span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="primary">0.002 g/mL</span><span data-alt-group="bsamilk" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">milkpowder</span>.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

3. {{< checkbox >}} Add 1 µL of the StrepMAB-Classic HRP conjugate antibody into the antibody dilution buffer.

### Membrane Processing and Blocking

1. {{< checkbox >}} Cut the membrane into pieces if different areas are to be stained with different antibodies and mark the pieces to always know their orientation, otherwise mark the whole membrane to always know it's orientation.
2. {{< checkbox >}} Place the nitrocellulose membrane into a tray on a tray shaker.
3. {{< checkbox >}} Add 20 ml blocking buffer and incubate @ RT with gentle agitation for 1 h or @ 4 °C with gentle agitation overnight.

{{< timer time="1:00:00" >}}

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
4. {{< checkbox >}} Replace the blocking buffer with 10 mL <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> and incubate @ RT with gentle agitation for 5 minutes.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

{{< timer time="0:05:00" >}}

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
5. {{< checkbox >}} Replace the <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> and incubate @ RT with gentle agitation for 5 minutes.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

{{< timer time="0:05:00" >}}

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
6. {{< checkbox >}} Replace the <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> and incubate @ RT with gentle agitation for 5 minutes.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

{{< timer time="0:05:00" >}}

### Antibody Binding

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
1. {{< checkbox >}} Replace the <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> with 12.5 ml of the diluted antibody and incubate @ RT with gentle agitation for 1h or @ 4 °C with gentle agitation overnight.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

{{< timer time="1:00:00" >}}

### Membrane Washing

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
1. {{< checkbox >}} Replace the diluted antibody with 10 mL <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> and incubate @ RT with gentle agitation for 5 minutes.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

{{< timer time="0:05:00" >}}

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
2. {{< checkbox >}} Replace the <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> and incubate @ RT with gentle agitation for 5 minutes.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

{{< timer time="0:05:00" >}}

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->
3. {{< checkbox >}} Replace the <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBST</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBST</span> with 10 mL <span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="primary">PBS</span><span data-alt-group="buffer" data-alt="secondary" style="display:none">TBS</span> and incubate @ RT with gentle agitation for 5 minutes.
<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->

{{< timer time="0:05:00" >}}

<!-- markdownlint-disable MD033 -->

<!-- markdownlint-enable MD033 -->
